Step-by-Step Guide: Ghirardelli Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 box Ghirardelli brownie mix
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup water

Step 1: Gather Ingredients

Collect all the ingredients needed for the Ghirardelli brownies.

Step 2: Preheat Oven

Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the brownie mix box.

Step 3: Add eggs

In a mixing bowl, add two eggs.

Step 4: Add vegtable oil

Add 1/3 cup of vegtable oil.

Step 5: Add water to bowl

Add 1/4 of water to bowl.

Step 6: Beat liquid ingredients together

Beat all of the liquid ingredients together.

Step 7: Add brownie mix to other ingredients

Add the brownie mix to the rest of the ingredients slowly.

Step 8: Spray pan

Spray the pan with Pam or any non-sticking spray.

Step 9: Add batter to the pan

Add the batter to the pan.

Step 10: After 30-35 minutes, enjoy!

After the brownies cook, cut them into squares and enjoy with the entire family!
