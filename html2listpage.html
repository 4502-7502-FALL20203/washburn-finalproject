Top 10 Marvel Movies

This article ranks the top 10 Marvel movies, listed in no particular order.

Black Panther

Director: Ryan Coogler
"Black Panther" is a Marvel superhero film that follows T'Challa, the newly crowned king of the fictional African nation of Wakanda, as he grapples with challenges to his throne. The movie explores themes of identity, heritage, and the responsibilities of leadership.

Avengers: Endgame

Director: Anthony and Joe Russo
"Avengers: Endgame" is the epic conclusion to the Marvel Cinematic Universe's Infinity Saga. The Avengers embark on a mission to undo the devastating effects of the Infinity War and face new challenges to save the universe.

Avengers: Infinity War

Director: Anthony and Joe Russo
In "Avengers: Infinity War," the Avengers and their allies must confront the powerful Thanos, who seeks to collect all the Infinity Stones to achieve his goal of wiping out half of all life in the universe.

Captain America: The Winter Soldier

Director: Anthony and Joe Russo
Captain America, along with Black Widow and Falcon, discovers a conspiracy within S.H.I.E.L.D. in "Captain America: The Winter Soldier." They face a formidable adversary known as the Winter Soldier.

Thor: Ragnarok

Director: Taika Waititi
"Thor: Ragnarok" takes a more comedic approach as Thor finds himself imprisoned on the planet Sakaar and must escape to prevent the destruction of his home world, Asgard, by the powerful Hela.

Iron Man

Director: Jon Favreau
The first film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, "Iron Man" introduces Tony Stark, a wealthy industrialist who builds a powered suit of armor and becomes the armored superhero Iron Man.

Guardians of the Galaxy

Director: James Gunn
"Guardians of the Galaxy" follows a group of intergalactic misfits—Star-Lord, Gamora, Drax, Rocket, and Groot—as they come together to prevent a powerful orb from falling into the wrong hands.

Spider-man: No Way Home

Director: Jon Watts
In "Spider-Man: No Way Home," Peter Parker's identity as Spider-Man is revealed, leading to a multiverse adventure with the help of Doctor Strange as they try to fix the chaotic consequences.

Captain America: Civil War

Director: Anthony and Joe Russo
"Captain America: Civil War" explores the ideological and personal conflicts between members of the Avengers as they are divided over government oversight and accountability.
